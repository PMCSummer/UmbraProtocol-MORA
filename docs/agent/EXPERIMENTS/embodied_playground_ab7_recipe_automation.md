# AB7 Recipe-Automation Probe (Embodied Playground)

## Scope
AB7 probe demonstrates integration of provisional recipe/precursor candidates into AB1-AB6 explanatory constraints without converting candidates into executable automation.

## Relation to P13/P14/P15
- P13 contributes delayed-credit/confounder/disconfirmation gating evidence.
- P14 contributes station affordance boundary refs.
- P15 contributes provisional recipe/precursor candidate records and lived trace lineage.

## Relation to AB1-AB6
- AB1/AB2/AB3 refs bind recipe candidates to open explanatory context.
- AB5 refs support update context.
- AB6 refs support attribution context.
- AB7 keeps all these as bounded evidence refs, not truth or execution authority.

## Claim boundary
AB7 keeps recipe/precursor candidates as constrained explanatory objects only:
- no mature recipe claim
- no automation execution
- no action candidate/AP01 request/world submission
- no consciousness/general-intelligence claim

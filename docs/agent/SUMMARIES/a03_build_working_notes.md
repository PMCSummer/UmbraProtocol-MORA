## Why A03 is not a module-name wrapper
A03 policy consumes typed candidate boundaries, invocation contracts, observation hooks, failure signatures, availability context, and A01/A02 typed packets. Non-tool boundaries are explicitly rejected; same-name different contracts are split.

## Why overbroad generic operations are not accepted
Candidates like `reason_about_anything` are rejected as overbroad generic operations instead of being canonicalized as broad competence claims.

## Where A02 linkage became tool-aware
A03 reads A02 gap entries and localizes only internal diagnostic/comparison/revalidation/simulation/inspection demand gaps into internal-tool missing/blocked/degraded linkage; world-action gaps are kept as world-action, not relabeled.
